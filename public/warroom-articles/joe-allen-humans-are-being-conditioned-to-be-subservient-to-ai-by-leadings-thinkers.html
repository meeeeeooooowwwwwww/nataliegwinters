Transhumanism editor Joe Allen talks to “War Room” host Steve Bannon about the rise of artificial intelligence. The segment begins with clips of author and psychologist Jordan Peterson talking about how the ChatGPT AI can solve almost any problem anyone puts before it. Bannon suggests that the move toward artificial intelligence suggests the equivalent of “blaspheming against the holy spirit” that the Bible warns against. Allen notes that “humans are getting dumber as the machines are getting smarter.” Check out Allen’s latest essay on the “Unholy Invasion” of chatboxes.
