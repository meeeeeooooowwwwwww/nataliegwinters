The 45th President of the United States Donald J. Trump joined Steve Bannon’s War Room to discuss his new book "Letters to Trump” which reflects on his many accomplishments and connections he has made with influential individuals. President Trump shared his friendship with the legendary athlete Arnold Palmer. He detailed the history behind Arnold Palmer’s partnership with Mark McCormack and the development of IMG. President Trump also shares the advice he gave to Palmer along with detailing how Palmer grew to become one of the nations most iconic golf champions.
