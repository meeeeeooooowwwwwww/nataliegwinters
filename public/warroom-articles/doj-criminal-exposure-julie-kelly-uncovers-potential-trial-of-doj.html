“So at least the southern Florida proceedings are going to be delayed until the New York trial is over. Now, there’s a good chance that the Southern Florida trial will be held before the election, and I get a sense from Judge Cannon that she really wants the DOJ to have to go on trial in Southern Florida,” Kelly said.

She added: “We’re not New York. We’re not Manhattan. We’re not in Washington, DC; we’re in Southern Florida, which is a good mix.  I think there’s a big part of her that wants  Jack Smith to put this case before a jury and just have the defense team eviscerate him and prove to the people what a manufactured case this was from the very beginning. So there is a chance that could still be pulled off,” Kelly told her co-host.
