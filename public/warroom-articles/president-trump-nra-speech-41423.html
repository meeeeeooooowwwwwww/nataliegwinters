President Trump delivered remarks at the National Rifle Association’s annual meeting in Indianapolis, Indiana. The President addressed the recent tragedies that occurred in Tennessee and Kentucky vowing to provide incentives for teachers to receive firearm training and strengthening laws to limit criminal activity.

“I was proud to be the most pro-gun, pro-Second Amendment president you’ve ever had in the White House and with your support in 2024, I will be your loyal friend and fearless champion once again as the 47th president of the United States.”
