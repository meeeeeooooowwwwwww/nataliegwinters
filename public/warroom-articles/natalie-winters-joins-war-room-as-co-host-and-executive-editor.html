Host Steve Bannon has added journalist Natalie Winters to the “War Room” as co-host and executive editor. Winters is already well-known to the “War Room” audience from her role as lead writer for the National Pulse. Her reporting now appear exclusively at War Room.org and will feature investigative pieces on China, the Chinese Communist Party, the Covid-19 pandemic and generally any government attempts to cover up the truth.
