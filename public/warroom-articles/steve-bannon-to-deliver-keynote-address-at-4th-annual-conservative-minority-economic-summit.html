Steve Bannon to Deliver Keynote Address at 4th Annual Conservative Minority Economic Summit

By KARI DONOVAN | 2025-03-13

Washington, D.C. – May 10, 2024 – Raynard Jackson & Associates LLC, in collaboration with Black Americans For A Better Future, proudly announces the 4th Annual Conservative Minority Economic Summit, themed “A Vision for Economic Opportunity and Empowerment in the 21st Century.”

Raynard Jackson is a frequent guest on the WarRoom program with Steve Bannon.

The summit is scheduled for Wednesday, May 22, 2024, from 12:00 p.m. to 9:00 p.m. at the prestigious Hilton Washington DC Capitol Hill.

This groundbreaking event will feature a lineup of distinguished speakers and panelists, including notable figures such as Stephen K. Bannon, host of War Room; Scott Bessent, Founder of Key Square Group; Dr. John Sibley Butler, Investor; Jennifer Carroll, Former Florida Lt. Governor; and Congressman Byron Donalds, U.S. Congressman representing FL-19, among others.

Additionally, the summit will showcase the participation of prominent individuals such as Stephen Gilchrist from the South Carolina Black Chamber of Commerce, Carvin Haggins, Multi-Grammy Award Winning Songwriter & Producer, Senator Trent Lott, Former Senate Majority Leader, Stephen Moore from the Committee to Unleash Prosperity, and Benny Pough, Music Recording Executive.

“We are honored to bring together such a diverse and influential group of speakers and participants for our 4th Annual Conservative Minority Economic Summit,” said Raynard Jackson, President & CEO of Raynard Jackson & Associates LLC.

“This event serves as a platform for fostering economic opportunity and empowerment within minority communities, and we are committed to driving meaningful change.”

Among the distinguished guests will be Ken Langone, founder of Home Depot.

Jackson further emphasized, “Our summit aims to facilitate networking and collaboration among conservative minority entrepreneurs, with a focus on building lasting relationships and driving business growth. We are thrilled to host the largest gathering of its kind in the country, bringing together top Black, Hispanic, Indian, and Asian businessmen, along with influential speakers dedicated to supporting minority entrepreneurs.”
