Matthew Tyrmand joins Steve Bannon and “War Room” at the AmericaFest in Phoenix, Arizona, where he reports on the status of the Brazilian people’s resistance to the fraudulent election in October.

“They are going to war on the streets. Last night, a source told me that the military tribunal… put an ultimatum to the Supreme Court and said you give us the machines by midnight last night, They ignored it. This gives them the pretext this week to act and invoke Article 142,” which allows the military to intervene. “If they do not win, the Southern Hemisphere is gone. China controls it and they’re coming for us next.”
