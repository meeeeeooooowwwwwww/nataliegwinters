Nebraska’s Electoral Vote at Stake: Eric Underwood Calls for Action to Secure CD2 for Trump on WarRoom

By KARI DONOVAN | 2025-03-13
Nebraska’s 2nd Congressional District holds a crucial electoral vote for President Trump, and Eric Underwood, who appeared on Monday's WarRoom...

Nebraska’s 2nd Congressional District holds a crucial electoral vote for President Trump, and Eric Underwood, who appeared on Monday’s WarRoom program, is rallying support to ensure victory. With over $15 million in Democratic funding targeting the race, Underwood stresses that grassroots efforts are key to maintaining control. Underwood asked people to join the Trump Get Out The Vote Efforts.

Nebraska’s Unique Electoral System Could Decide the Election

Eric Underwood, a congressional candidate in Nebraska, is raising the alarm about the importance of Nebraska’s 2nd Congressional District (CD2) in the upcoming 2024 election. Nebraska is one of the few states that splits its electoral votes by Congressional district, and CD2, which includes Omaha, is fiercely contested. Underwood emphasizes, "This district doesn’t just decide a Congressional seat. It’s tied directly to one critical electoral vote for President Trump.”

Democrats Pour Millions Into Tight Race

The stakes are high in CD2, with Republican Congressman Don Bacon defending his seat in a highly competitive race. Underwood points out, "The Democrats have dumped over $15 million into this district, and they’re bringing in the same candidate to challenge Bacon again.” He underscores the strategic importance of this electoral vote, explaining, "In a race that could come down to 270 versus 268 electoral votes, losing CD2 would be a devastating blow.”

Voter Turnout Is Key to Victory

Underwood stresses the importance of voter mobilization. "Everything now is about getting out the vote,” he says, encouraging Trump supporters to engage in grassroots efforts. The Trump Force 47 initiative, along with county Republican parties, is spearheading voter outreach in key areas like Douglas, Sarpy, and Saunders counties. "These local efforts are putting boots on the ground, going door-to-door, connecting directly with voters,” Underwood adds.

Dark Money and Globalist Influence

Underwood also raises concerns about outside influence in the race, citing significant contributions from wealthy donors and special interest groups. "We’re seeing dark money from George Soros and globalist PACs flooding this race,” he warns. Underwood views this influx of Democratic funding as an effort to tip the scales in a race that could impact the national electoral outcome.

Why CD2 Matters for Trump’s Re-election

Underwood believes that winning CD2 is crucial for Trump’s re-election. "This election could very well come down to one electoral vote, and Nebraska is in a unique position,” he explains. With Nebraska’s electoral votes split between statewide results and individual Congressional districts, a win in CD2 could give Trump an essential edge in a close election.

Call to Action: Boots on the Ground

To combat the Democratic push in CD2, Underwood is calling on Trump supporters to rally behind local Republican efforts. He encourages people to get involved through social media platforms like Trump Force 47 and to volunteer in local get-out-the-vote campaigns. "This is where the fight is,” Underwood says, urging the War Room audience to engage in the final stretch before Election Day.

In closing, Underwood left a stark reminder of what’s at stake: "This election is too close to leave any vote on the table. CD2 could be the deciding factor in 2024.”
